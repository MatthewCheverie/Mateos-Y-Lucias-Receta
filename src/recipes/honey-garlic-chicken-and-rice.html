honey garlic chicken

Description

A delicious honey garlic chicken recipe

Ingredients

  • panko
  • corn starch
  • choice of oil to fry
  • chicken thigh
  • eggs
  • pepper
  • salt
  • parsley
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • honey
  • soy suace
  • minced galric
  • optional cook white rice to serve along with

Steps

  1. Prep: Chop the bacon, add water and salt to a pot, combine the egg yolks / egg whites / grated parmesan into a mixing bowl
  2. Reduce the bacon and crisp ir in a skillet and bring the water to a boil
  3. Once bacon reduce remove majoirty of the grease and seperate most of the bacon for garniushing later. Let pasta boil in water for 10 minutes
  4. Once pasta is done add it into the skillet with remaining bacon and grease along with some pasta water and the cheese yolk mixture
  5. Stir until desired consistency is reached may need to add more pasta water
  6. Once done plate it in a bowl and garnish with some of the bacon you separated
